GATE

GATE is a gateway to various postgraduate courses in engineering and science offered by IITs, NITs, IIITs, and other leading institutions. It also serves as a screening test for various public sector undertakings (PSUs) like BHEL, ONGC, IOCL, etc. Many private sector companies also consider GATE score for recruitment.

GRE

GRE is a crucial exam for students aspiring to study abroad, especially in the United States. It is accepted by thousands of graduate and business schools across the world. Many scholarships, fellowships, and assistantships also consider GRE scores for selection.We have all the essential free resources by deeply researching to crack it.


GMAT

The GMAT exam is important for students aspiring to pursue a graduate or business degree, as it is widely accepted by over 7,000 programs offered by more than 2,300 universities and institutions in more than 110 countries. Many scholarships, fellowships, and assistantships also consider GMAT scores for selection.

CAT

The CAT exam is important for students aspiring to pursue a management degree, as it is widely accepted by over 1200 management institutes in India. Many top B-schools, including the IIMs, consider CAT scores for selection.
