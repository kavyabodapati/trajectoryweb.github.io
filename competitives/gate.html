GATE ROADMAP

Welcome to our GATE exam resources and roadmaps section! Here, we have compiled all the necessary information, tips, and tricks to help you prepare for the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) exam.

GATE is a national level entrance examination conducted by the Indian Institute of Technology (IITs) and Indian Institute of Science (IISc) on a rotational basis. The exam is primarily conducted to test the comprehensive understanding of undergraduate level engineering and science students in various fields.
