Backend Roadmap

Backend development is the part of web development that focuses on building and maintaining the server-side of web applications. It involves creating the infrastructure, databases, and APIs that power the web application. Here is a backend roadmap that covers all of the essential skills and technologies to learn in order to become a successful backend developer.
