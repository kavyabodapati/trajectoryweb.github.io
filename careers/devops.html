DevOps Roadmap

DevOps is a software development approach that emphasizes collaboration between development and operations teams to automate and streamline the software delivery process. It involves the integration of development, testing, deployment, and operations processes. Here is a DevOps roadmap that covers all the essential skills and technologies to learn in order to become a successful DevOps engineer.
